Featured Project

GameScraper

GameScraper lets you compare the prices of video games across 4 major online retailers. The prices are centered around Steam, with the other 3 websites offering potentially lower prices. However, if a game is on sale in the Steam store, then that'll be the lowest price you can get.

The design is straightforward: users connect to a reverse proxy that rate-limits, caches, and load balances requests across multiple web servers. A web scraper - well, scrapes - and updates the database once a day.
